05 — FAQ

Questions, answered.

How much does a Zoho implementation cost?
It depends on scope. A focused automation or integration can start from a few hundred dollars, while a full multi‑app Zoho implementation is quoted per project. Book a free consultation and you'll get a clear, fixed quote — no hourly surprises.
Can you migrate my data from HubSpot, Salesforce or Monday.com?
Yes. I run full migrations into Zoho from HubSpot, Salesforce, Monday.com, GoHighLevel and others — with field mapping, de‑duplication and validation so nothing is lost or duplicated.
Do you work with US and UK businesses remotely?
Yes. I work fully remote with SMBs across the US and UK and am comfortable collaborating across those time zones over call, email and shared docs.
How long does a typical Zoho project take?
Small automations and integrations usually take a few days to two weeks. Full CRM implementations typically run a few weeks depending on the number of apps, integrations and data to migrate.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. Beyond project delivery I offer monthly support retainers for changes, new automations, monitoring and training as your processes evolve.
Which Zoho apps do you work with?
25+ across the suite — including CRM, Bigin, Books, Invoice, Inventory, Creator, Flow, Desk, Campaigns, Sites, Analytics and more.
Can you integrate Zoho with telephony, accounting or other tools I already use?
Yes. I connect Zoho with telephony and SMS (RingCentral, Twilio, Google Voice, ClickSend, SMS Magic), accounting and ERP (QuickBooks, NetSuite, SAP) and automation platforms (Zapier, Zoho Flow), plus custom REST API integrations.
Are you a certified Zoho partner?
I'm an independent Zoho developer and consultant with 3.5+ years building on the platform — not a badged partner agency. That means you work directly with the person doing the work, with no account‑manager layer in between.
